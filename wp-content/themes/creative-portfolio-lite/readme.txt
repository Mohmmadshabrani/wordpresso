== Description ==

Creative Portfolio Lite is a free WordPress theme for designing personal portfolios, artist portfolios, designer portfolios, contemporary well as freelancer portfolios. It is also useful for creating business portfolios as well. It is a creative theme with a sophisticated design and elegant layout that perfectly represents your skills as well as business objective through your website. It is beautiful and makes use of responsive design to make a website that scales perfectly across the screens of various devices. Minimal design highlights the important information. As this theme is made retina-ready, all your information and imagery will be clearly shown. A user-friendly theme interface brings ease for beginners and eliminates the need to write codes in the process. A wonderful banner is there along with sections such as Team, testimonial, etc. to show various information. Its animations and CSS effects will add more to your website resulting in a Creative Portfolio Lite. Secure and clean codes are added to make the design lightweight, streamlined, and bring Call to Action Buttons (CTA) that will make conversions possible. It is a Bootstrap-based theme giving faster page load time and has customization options. This modern theme also brings a translation-ready design and includes social media icons for your website.
